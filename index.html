About the Teacher

Fun lessons in a friendly atmosphere.

Hello, my name is Shin-Yin Estes. I’ve been providing students of all skill levels with professional piano training for more than 25 years. I believe it’s difficult for those looking to learn piano to find a teacher that can guide at any skill level, from absolute beginner to concert-ready. It’s rare to find someone with a high-quality music education, actual experience performing in front of large audiences, and years of practical experience teaching. It is my goal to take these accomplishments and put them at your disposal to give you the personalized piano education that you or your loved ones deserve.

A strong understanding of music is essential to learning any instrument. I take special care to make sure my beginner students form a solid musical foundation that they can build upon for years to come. I teach in many styles of music, such as classical, jazz, and pop.  I offer lessons to people of all ages and all levels of experience.  Why not give yourself or your family a lifetime of musical enrichment?

Lessons and event booking are available to Camden county New Jersey and surrounding areas, including eastern Philadelphia.  For specific questions about areas of coverage please contact the teacher.

Shin-Yin Estes is a member of the Philharmonic of South Jersey, the New Jersey Music Teachers Association, and the National Guild of Piano Teachers. Winner of numerous piano competitions, she graduated in 1983 from the Philadelphia College of the Performing Arts (Univ. of the Arts). She has been honored with Who’s Who among students in American universities and colleges. Shin-Yin has been teaching piano in a personalized manner for over 25 years. She has a passion for teaching and a unique musical outlook; both will help you or your loved ones realize a sense of musical potential and a personal love of music.

For those who speak Mandarin Chinese, Shin-Yin is bilingual, and can teach completely in Mandarin if preferred.

Lesson Info & Registration

Payment

  • Student fees are due on the first week of each month.
  • The whole month will need to be paid for at this time.
  • Beginner lessons are $40 per half hour, $60 for a 45 minute session, or $80 per hour.
  • Advanced lessons are $50 per half hour, $75 for a 45 minute session, or $100 per hour.

Cancellations

Lesson sessions can be rescheduled if given 24 hours advance notice.

Registration

Students may register at any time. There is no fee to complete the registration process.  Please fill out the enrollment form below to begin the registration process. Alternatively, you may email the teacher directly at shin.estes@gmail.com or call her at (856)-904-8499.
