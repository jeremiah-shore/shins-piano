Meet Your Teacher

Hello, my name is Shin-Yin Estes.

It's my goal to give you the personalized piano education that you or your loved ones deserve.

Playing piano can be tough. Luckily, I'm here to help with that!

Lesson Info

Pricing

Beginner lessons start at $40 per half hour. Advanced lessons start at $50 per half hour.

Payment

Student fees are due on the first week of each month. Lessons are paid for a month at a time.

Registration

Register at any time; there is no fee to complete the registration process.

Cancellations

Lesson sessions can be rescheduled if given 24 hours advance notice.
