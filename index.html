Lesson Info & Registration

Payment

  • Student fees are due on the first week of each month.
  • The whole month will need to be paid for at this time.
  • Beginner lessons are $40 per half hour, $60 for a 45 minute session, or $80 per hour.
  • Advanced lessons are $50 per half hour, $75 for a 45 minute session, or $100 per hour.

Cancellations

Lesson sessions can be rescheduled if given 24 hours advance notice.

Registration

Students may register at any time. There is no fee to complete the registration process.  Please fill out the enrollment form below to begin the registration process. Alternatively, you may email the teacher directly at shin.estes@gmail.com or call her at (856)-904-8499.
